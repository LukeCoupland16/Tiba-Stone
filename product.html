Freestanding Bathtub

Forma bath

marble bathtub

A sculptural expression of design and carving. Conceived as a monolithic vessel, the Forma Bath is carved from a single block of Calacatta Viola marble — its sweeping silhouette designed to cradle both body and light.

Designed for Wellness

Wrapped in Marble: A Bathtub Designed for Wellness

The Forma Bath transforms the daily ritual of bathing into an act of contemplation. Its generous interior dimensions and ergonomic contours are the result of extensive research into the relationship between body, water, and stone.

Handcrafted Marble Bathtub made in Italy by Tiba Stone

Each Forma is sculpted by master artisans in our Carrara atelier using processes that combine advanced manufacturing with generations of craft knowledge. The exterior retains a subtle honed texture; the interior is polished to a mirror finish.

Available in five marbles
