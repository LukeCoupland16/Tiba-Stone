Our Philosophy

Where Nature Meets Intention

At Tiba Stone, we believe that marble is not merely a building material — it is a testament to geological time, a record of pressure, heat, and patience written across millennia. Our role is not to impose upon it, but to listen.

Every block that enters our atelier carries its own character: veining that cannot be replicated, colour shifts that belong to one quarry and one moment alone. We honour this singularity through unhurried craft, bespoke collaboration, and a refusal to treat any two commissions alike.

The result is stone that feels inevitable — as though it was always meant to take this form.

The Process

From Quarry to Masterpiece

01 — Material Selection

Chosen at
the Source

Every Tiba Stone commission begins at the quarry. Our master selectors travel to extraction sites across Italy, Greece, and beyond, reading the face of each block for veining character, structural integrity, and tonal warmth. Only stone that meets our standard of singularity is chosen — material worthy of the hands that will shape it.

02 — Design

Drawn from
Dialogue

Each piece is conceived in close conversation with the client — architect, designer, or homeowner. We translate spatial requirements and aesthetic vision into precise technical drawings, always respecting the natural geometry of the chosen stone. The design honours what the marble wants to become.

03 — Cutting & Shaping

Precision
Meets Patience

Modern diamond-wire technology and traditional hand tools work in concert. Our artisans rough-cut each block to within millimetres of the final form, then refine by hand — a process that cannot be rushed. The stone reveals its deepest character only as material is slowly, deliberately removed.

04 — Hand Finishing

The Touch
That Defines

Finishing is where craft becomes art. Through successive stages of honing, sanding, and polishing, our artisans coax a luminous depth from the stone’s surface. Whether the brief calls for a mirror polish, a soft hone, or a textured brushed finish, every pass is made by hand and judged by eye.

05 — Quality & Inspection

Nothing
Leaves Unseen

Before any piece departs our atelier, it undergoes a meticulous inspection under natural light. Surfaces are examined for uniformity, edges for precision, and the overall form for fidelity to the original design. We sign our name only to work that meets the standard we would accept in our own homes.

Our Sources

Stone Without Borders

We seek the finest stone the earth has to offer — travelling to quarries across continents, building lasting relationships with extraction partners who share our reverence for the material.

Carrara

Tuscany, Italy

The storied quarries of the Apuan Alps, yielding marble prized since antiquity for its luminous white ground and delicate grey veining.

Thassos

Macedonia, Greece

An Aegean island renowned for its crystalline white marble — pure, translucent, and untouched by veining, ideal for sculptural forms.

Afyon

Anatolia, Turkey

Rich cream and honey-toned marbles from the heart of Anatolia, prized for their warmth and depth of colour in interior settings.

Markina

Basque Country, Spain

Home to the legendary Nero Marquina — a deep black marble veined with white, offering dramatic contrast and timeless sophistication.

Tivoli

Lazio, Italy

The ancient travertine quarries near Rome, yielding stone with warm, earthy tones and a natural texture that ages with extraordinary grace.

Alicante

Valencia, Spain

Source of the celebrated Emperador and Crema Marfil — warm, versatile stones that bring understated richness to every application.
