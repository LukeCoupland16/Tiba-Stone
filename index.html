Bespoke Marble Atelier

For over two decades, Tiba Stone has transformed the world's most extraordinary marble into objects of enduring beauty. Each piece begins as a conversation — between client and artisan, between vision and stone.

We believe that marble is not merely a material but a living record of the earth's memory. Our atelier honours this legacy through patient handcraft, bespoke design, and an unwavering commitment to the singular.
