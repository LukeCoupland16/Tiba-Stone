Dining Tables

Bronze

bespoke stone dining table

Bronze takes its name from the warm mineral depth that certain stones carry — that quality of age and weight that makes a piece feel as though it has always been in the room. A dining table of quiet gravitas, designed to anchor and endure.

The Table

Weight, Warmth, Authority

Bronze is a table you feel before you sit at it. The stone has a presence in the room that other materials rarely achieve — a quality of permanence, of having always been there. It is generous in proportion and deeply calm in character, inviting long meals and unhurried conversation without demanding attention.

The Character of Deep Stone

The stone selections for Bronze were chosen for their depth — veining that carries warmth and mineral complexity. This is not a table for the cool and clinical. It suits rooms that have lived in them, walls that have absorbed years of light. Bronze improves the longer it is used; the surface gathers the subtle patina of gathered life.

Available in six stones

Stone & Surface

Bronze is offered in Calacatta Viola, Fendy Grey, Ice Age Onyx, Noir Aziza, Rosso Levanto, and Verde Patricia. Each stone brings its own character — from the dramatic veining of Calacatta Viola to the deep silence of Noir Aziza.

Bronze is selected specifically for stones with warm, deep mineral character — veining that reads as ancient and layered rather than purely graphic. Where some of our dining tables lean architectural, Bronze leans residential and warm. The proportions are generous, the form unhurried. It suits spaces where the dining table is meant to feel like the heart of the home.

For dining tables, where the full surface will be seen every day, slab selection is especially important — and we spend time on it with every client. All dining surfaces are sealed before delivery. A honed finish is recommended for its durability and ease of maintenance. We provide a comprehensive stone care guide — routine wiping, avoidance of acids, and periodic resealing to maintain the surface over time. The stone improves with use; a dining table does not need to be precious.

Stone dining tables require careful logistics — we coordinate white-glove delivery and installation as part of every order. A site survey is conducted before delivery to confirm access and floor structure. Our team handles placement and levelling; the table arrives ready for use.
